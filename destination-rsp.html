NISEKO, HOKKAIDO

WELCOME TO NISEKO, JAPAN'S MOST CONTEMPORARY SKI AREA

Experience the best snow in the world, savour the delicacies of Japanese cuisine, adventure into an extraordinary landscape of dramatic coastlines and mighty volcanoes, while you take in Niseko's fascinating culture and friendly hospitality.

What we do best - Welcome to Niseko, the birth place of Alteo and Japan's most contemporary and exclusive ski area. Tucked away in the southeast corner of the island of mountainous Hokkaido, Japanese culture and hospitality meet here to create a world class skiing environment. Alteo has dedicated years into refining this experience, a perfect balance of culture, adventure and luxury in Japan’s snowy capital. Your time in Niseko will include: skiing on arguably the best snow in the world, an exploration of the jewels of Japanese cuisine and breathtaking views of the Hokkaido landscape, coastline and volcanos.
