NISEKO, HOKKAIDO

WELCOME TO NISEKO, JAPAN'S MOST CONTEMPORARY SKI AREA

Experience the best snow in the world, savour the delicacies of Japanese cuisine, adventure into an extraordinary landscape of dramatic coastlines and mighty volcanoes, while you take in Niseko's fascinating culture and friendly hospitality.

What we do best - Welcome to Niseko, the birth place of Alteo and Japan's most contemporary and exclusive ski area. Tucked away in the southwest corner of mountainous island of Hokkaido, Japanese culture and hospitality meet here to create a world class skiing environment. Alteo has dedicated years into refining this experience, a perfect balance of culture, adventure and luxury in Japan’s snowy capital. Your time in Niseko will include: skiing on arguably the best snow in the world, an exploration of the jewels of Japanese cuisine and breathtaking views of the Hokkaido landscape, coastline and volcanos.

Early December

The season kicks off with the opening of the ski resort around the first week of December. This is a great time for beginner skiers to learn stress free due to the lack of people on the slopes. Likewise, enjoy strolls through a specious Hirafu, eat at your favourite restaurants without the hassle of fighting for a reservation and watch as the landscape transforms from green to white.

Late December to Mid January

Come experience Niseko at its peak. As temperatures drop and the snow accumulates, so does tourism, bringing us to a pinacle during the last week of the month. As we enter January, enjoy nightly top ups of fresh snow as well as a vibrant and electric town life. The izakayas are smoking, the beer pouring and the energy in town is palpable.

Mid January to Mid February

Winter is in full effect, the Sapporo Snow & Ice festival is on and so is the snow tap Japan is renowned for. This is your time for the full Japow experience. Come and experience life in white. Everywhere you look, snow will persist, creating beautiful white landscapes and even better skiing. Those wanting to experience deep powder skiing or those simply wanting to submerge in a winter wonderland, this is your time.

Mid February to March

The sun starts to break through allowing for beautiful days on the slopes with views of Mt. Yotei. The town and slopes begin to quiet down and to give the resort a more intimate feel. This is a great time of the winter for beginners and experts alike. Beginners can enjoy beautiful skiing weather (sunshine) and clear views of the surrounding landscape while more advanced skiers can adventure further with a backcountry guide.

March to end of Season

Spring is here. Quiet slopes with warm ski conditions makes for a very pleasant learning experience. This is the time for late starts to the day and Apres ski lifestyle in the evenings. Indulge in your favourite restaurants and accommodations, take a road trip to the less visited parts of the island to enjoy the rugged and volcanic coastlines of Hokkaido.
