Niseko, Hokkaido, North Japan

Welcome to Niseko, Japan's most contemporary ski area

Experience the best snow in the world, savour the delicacies of Japanese cuisine, adventure into an extraordinary landscape of dramatic coastlines and mighty volcanoes, while you take in Niseko's fascinating culture and friendly hospitality.

What we do best - Welcome to Niseko, the birth place of Alteo and Japan's most contemporary and exclusive ski area. Tucked away in the southeast corner of the island of mountainous Hokkaido, Japanese culture and hospitality meet here to create a world class skiing environment. Alteo has dedicated years into refining this experience, a perfect balance of culture, adventure and luxury in Japan’s snowy capital. Your time in Niseko will include: skiing on arguably the best snow in the world, an exploration of the jewels of Japanese cuisine and breathtaking views of the Hokkaido landscape, coastline and volcanos.

Early December

The season kicks off, empty slopes as the town fills up towards the end of the month.

Late December to Mid January

This is when Niseko town is in full bloom, JAPOW is in full effect with nightly top ups of snow.

Mid January to Mid February

CNY - we are in the heart of winter. The Sapporo Snow/Ice festival is on and so is the snow tap. This is your time for the full Japow experience.

Mid February to March

The sun starts to break through allowing for beautiful days on the slopes with views of Mt. Yotei. The town and slopes are quieter and feel more private.

March to end of Season

Spring is here. Quiet slopes with warm ski conditions makes for a very pleasant learning experience. It is the time for late starts and post ski Apres.

NISEKO Travel Services

Private Ski Lessons and Guiding

Individual and family group classes to improve your skill. Child specialist instructors to get the young to fall in love with the slopes. Attentive mountain guidance to help you reach the finest locations.

Luxury Accommodation and Lodging

Premier accommodation at Japanese modern villas, top hotels and traditional ryokans, all with access to private onsen for families, couples and individuals. Exceptional fully serviced luxury lodging for elegant seclusion and enjoyment.

Local Culture and Tradition

Captivating cultural traditions to reveal during your journey. Sophisticated tea ceremonies, delicate Geisha experiences, untamed Sumo showdowns. Exclusive access to top nightclubs or relaxing massage and yoga sessions at the most delicate spas.

Japanese Cuisine and Spirits

Exquisite kaiseki haute cuisine and modern japanese dining, combined with flavoursome secret local eateries, masterfully distilled beverages and craft beer. Select restaurants, private in-house dining and hidden street-food gems.

Full Concierge and Private Transportation

Personalised excellence both on and off-snow, shopping companion and 24hr full concierge services during the complete duration of your trip. Chauffeur services and private transportation on a carefully selected vehicle fleet.

Adventure Journeys and Activities

Off the beaten track day road trips to remote volcanic baths, horseback riding and ice fishing. Adventurous CAT skiing and Off-Piste backcountry tours. Heliski runs to yet untouched snow powder paradises.

Your joy is our inspiration.

Alteo guests can look forward to an effortless and exhilarating Niseko winter getaway, infused with both luxury and discovery.
