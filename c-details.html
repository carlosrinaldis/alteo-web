December

The season kicks off, empty slopes as the town fills up towards the end of the month.

January

This is when Niseko town is in full bloom, JAPOW is in full effect with nightly top ups of snow.

February

CNY - we are in the heart of winter. The Sapporo Snow/Ice festival is on and so is the snow tap. This is your time for the full Japow experience.

March

The sun starts to break through allowing for beautiful days on the slopes with views of Mt. Yotei. The town and slopes are quieter and feel more private.
